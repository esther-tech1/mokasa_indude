Heavy Jacket

Heavy jackets, weighing over 4-6 pounds, are designed for extreme cold and maximum protection, often featuring thick insulation, dense materials (wool, waxed cotton), and fur-lined hoods. They offer superior durability and warmth, making them essential for harsh winters. Common types include heavy winter puffer jackets, insulated work jackets, and robust wool jackets.They typically weigh between 4 to 6 pounds or more, providing substantial warmth, often using dense, water-resistant fabrics and thick insulation like 750+ fill-power down or heavy synthetic materials.
